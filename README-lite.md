# Customer Churn & Revenue Retention Analytics

**Industry:** Telecommunications  
**Role Focus:** Data Analyst | Business Intelligence | Revenue Analytics  
**Core Stack:** SQL | Python | Power BI | Data Modeling | Analytics Engineering

---

## Executive Snapshot
This project analyzes **customer churn as a revenue risk problem**, quantifying
**monthly recurring revenue (MRR) at risk** and delivering a
**revenue-weighted retention prioritization framework**.

The analysis integrates **churn risk indicators**, **customer lifecycle
segmentation**, and **financial exposure modeling** to support
**executive-level decision making**.

---

## Business Value Delivered
- Quantification of **revenue at risk due to churn**
- Identification of **early-tenure and contract-based churn drivers**
- Revenue concentration and Pareto analysis
- Retention prioritization based on **financial impact**
- Executive-ready Power BI dashboard
- Independent SQL and Python validation

---

## Key Insight
> Medium-risk customers represent the largest share of **preventable revenue
exposure**, making early intervention more impactful than late-stage churn
mitigation.
